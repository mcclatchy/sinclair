GETTING STARTED

Before you start your project, please take a moment to contact the design group. We can help you configure your story properly in Content Studio, discuss any necessary changes to workflow, recommend solutions to common problems and develop solutions for new problems.

v2

Sinclair v2 takes a different approach to special projects. Instead of building external pages largely reliant on JavaScript, this version provides a thin style layer to enhance our current publishing system. This approach provides the following benefits:

  • A smaller impact to the regular workflow.
  • A better search engine ranking.
  • Better social media integration.
  • It does not affect Omniture data.
  • It does not affect reporter data.
  • Standard revenue elements can be included.
  • It reduces development costs.
  • Stories can be shared between markets.
